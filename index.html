AI Voice Automation · Retell AI + n8n + Supabase

GlowUp Hair Salon AI Voice Receptionist

Built a fully working AI voice receptionist for GlowUp Hair Salon using Retell AI and GPT-4.1. The AI answers customer calls, collects caller information, and books appointments. After each call, an n8n automation workflow is triggered that extracts call data, creates a Google Calendar event, logs the call to Google Sheets, saves it to Supabase, and sends a Discord notification. The entire system is deployed live on Railway.
